About

He builds what
replaces the problem.

Mark Wexler is a social entrepreneur, keynote speaker, author, and Visiting Research Fellow at King's College London.

Over twenty years, across six continents and 33 countries, Mark has co-founded organizations using nearly every legal and capital structure available to a founder: nonprofits, for-profits, cooperatives, foundations, and social enterprises. None of it was built alone.

The through-line never changes. Rather than protest a broken system, he builds the enterprise that makes it obsolete, then hands the model to the people closest to the problem. Build something that replaces the problem, and the rest follows.
