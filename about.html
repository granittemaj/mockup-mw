In briefTwenty years across six continents and 33 countries

Over twenty years, Mark has co-founded organizations using nearly every legal and capital structure available to a founder: nonprofits, for-profits, cooperatives, foundations, and social enterprises. None of it was built alone.

Rather than protest a broken system, he builds the enterprise that makes it obsolete, then hands the model to the people closest to the problem.
