What we collect, why, and the choices you have.

Last updated · July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit markwexler.com (the “site”), how it is used, and the choices you have. Mark Wexler is the data controller for this site.

Information we collect

Information you give us

When you use the contact form, we collect the details you submit: your name, email address, organization, inquiry type, event or engagement details, location, audience size, and your message. When you sign up to be notified about the Changemaker Chronicles, we collect your email address.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, we collect standard technical data such as your browser type, device, approximate location, referring pages, and how you interact with the site. This is gathered through the analytics tools described below.

How we use your information

  • To respond to speaking, consulting, and media inquiries.
  • To send you launch updates you have asked to receive, and to let you unsubscribe at any time.
  • To understand how the site is used and to improve it.
  • To keep the site secure and prevent spam and abuse.

Analytics

With your consent, we use Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors use the site. These tools set cookies and may collect usage data, including anonymized interaction and session information. They load only after you accept analytics cookies. See our Cookie Notice for details and how to change your choice.

Email

We use Brevo to deliver form notifications and any updates you subscribe to. Your email address and message are processed by Brevo on our behalf under their own privacy terms.

Spam protection

Our forms are protected by Google reCAPTCHA, which is subject to the Google Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Data retention

We keep inquiry and subscription data only as long as needed to respond to you and to maintain a record of our correspondence, then delete it.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or to object to its processing. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

Contact

For any privacy question or request, please reach out through our contact page.
