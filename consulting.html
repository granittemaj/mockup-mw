How it works
01

Run the SWOT

A Montara Circle runs a SWOT analysis on a specific problem: the real strengths to leverage, the real weaknesses to mitigate.

02

Design & compete

A hand-picked group is asked to design an innovative, replicable solution. Teams compete through structured exercises.

03

Vote & commit

At the end, the group votes. And the organization commits to building the winning idea.

It is not a brainstorm. It is a commitment engine. The output is not a report. It is a decision the organization has agreed to act on.

The outputWhat a Montara Circle produces

A new product concept. A strategic pivot. An innovation pipeline. A culture reset.

The Montara Circle has produced companies that raised tens of millions in venture capital and changed the economic conditions of entire regions. It can be scaled to the time you have, from a focused session to a multi-day intensive, and deployed for a leadership team, a startup ecosystem, or an organization at an inflection point.
