01 — The Discovery

He built a beverage company that made Brazil nuts more valuable to communities in the Peruvian Amazon than forced labor ever could. A restaurant in Amsterdam that gave survivors careers. A children's village in Thailand from a burned-out hut and ten thousand dollars in cash.

In each case the solution was not a report, a campaign, or a rescue operation. It was a business. Build something that makes the problem economically irrational, and the rest follows. His talks don't make you feel inspired for a day. They give you a framework for building something that lasts.

The Framework

A vocabulary that doesn't exist anywhere else.

Forged in the Amazon, Amsterdam, and the Golden Triangle. Not in a seminar room.

01

Out-create

Fight injustice not by opposing it, but by building something better that makes it obsolete.

02

Pragmatic Idealism

Dream at the scale of the problem. Act with the discipline the problem demands.

03

Ruthless Execution

Choose the direction before the speed. Then refuse to stop.

04

Aggressive Humility

Act on the evidence that what you built isn't working, before sunk cost hides the truth.

05

Impact Stack

Root cause, not symptom. What you have, not what you wish. What the problem demands.

06

The Montara Circle

A two-day convening that produces a committed outcome. It produced REBBL, then Dignita.
